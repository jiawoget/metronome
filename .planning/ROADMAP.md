## Overview

This v1.0 staging roadmap is a completed-only semantic import of legacy history into native OpenGSD. Eight verified legacy packs are represented by one completed compact import plan and summary each. Exactly 83 verified slice identities remain traceable as historical evidence; the five unfinished pack boundaries and their 49 not-started slice decomposition remain frozen proposals under `docs/v1/`, not current or future phases.
